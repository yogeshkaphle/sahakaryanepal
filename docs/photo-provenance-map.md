## Two things to flag before wiring

### 1. Resolution
These photos are small, compressed images inside report layouts. Fine for a card thumbnail or an inline project image. **Likely too low-res for a full-bleed hero.** Check actual pixel dimensions of the 19 extracted files before designing around them — if they're under ~1200px wide, the hero stays photo-optional as the design system already specifies.

### 2. Named individuals vs group activity shots
Two different categories:
- **Activity/group photos** (trainings, distributions, meetings, enterprise sites) — the 14 captioned above.
- **Named-story portraits** (Khinchalal Pahari, Chandrakumari) — a person's face attached to their name, village, and personal circumstances (migration history, poverty, business failure).

Use the first freely. For the second, the caption must match what the org published, and it's worth confirming these two are happy to appear on the public site specifically — they're the only photos where an individual is identifiable *by name* alongside personal history.
